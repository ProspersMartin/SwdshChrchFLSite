WEDDINGS!

Getting married is one of the truly great events in life. Perhaps a romantic wedding at one of Florida's beautiful beaches may be something for you?

 

The Swedish Church in Florida has the right to perform marriages under American law, which means that Swedish citizens can simultaneously enter into marriage according to

the Swedish church, and to US law. The ceremony is legally valid everywhere.

 

Where can you get married?

 

You can get married either inside the church or under one of the oak trees in the garden at the Scandinavian Center in Davie.

 

A beach wedding under palm trees or by the water's also an option.

What does it cost?

 

A Wedding in the Church, or Church garden cost $625 USD.

If you would like a Wedding on the beach the cost is $750 USD.

 

Book your wedding by paying the non-refundable deposit of $300 and email us your full name, social security number, address, email addresses and telephone numbers.

 

When the deposit is paid, your wedding date is reserved. You can change the date at no additional cost. However, if you wish to cancel, please remember that the deposit is non-refundable.
